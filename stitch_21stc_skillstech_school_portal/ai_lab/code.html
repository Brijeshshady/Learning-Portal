Absolutely. Imagine you are standing on a foggy mountain and trying to find your way down to the lowest valley. Because it's foggy, you can't see the whole landscape; you can only feel the slope of the ground directly beneath your feet.

Gradient Descent is an algorithm that does exactly this mathematically:

  1. Check the slope (Calculate the Gradient): Figure out which direction is downhill.
  2. Take a step (Learning Rate): Move a small distance in that downhill direction.
  3. Repeat: Keep doing this until the ground is flat (you've reached the minimum).
